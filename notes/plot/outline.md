# Story Outline - The Last Lighthouse

## Three-Act Structure

### Act 1 - Setup (25% / ~21,000 words)
**Goal**: Establish Elena's isolation, discover the journal, establish stakes

#### Opening Image
- Elena performing routine lighthouse maintenance
- Her isolation is deliberate, self-imposed
- Brief hints at "the mainland" and what she's avoiding

#### Inciting Incident (Chapter 3-4)
- Storm damages tower, reveals hidden compartment
- Elena discovers Captain Archer's 1891 journal
- First mention of "The Perseverance" - a ship not in official records

#### First Plot Point (End of Act 1)
- Elena finds newspaper clipping in brother's apartment
- Dylan was researching the exact same ship
- His notes stop abruptly three years ago
- Decision: She will investigate, breaking her self-imposed isolation

### Act 2 - Confrontation (50% / ~42,000 words)
**Goal**: Rising investigation, Elena's past revealed, mystery deepens

#### Rising Action
- Elena travels to mainland archives
- Introduction of supporting characters (archivist, local historian, coast guard)
- Flashbacks reveal Dylan's disappearance circumstances
- More journal entries: Captain Archer's mission grows stranger

#### Midpoint
- Elena discovers Dylan's storage unit
- Contains extensive research, maritime charts, photographs
- Final entry: "I found it. The coordinates are real. Going tonight."
- Coordinates point to specific spot offshore from lighthouse

#### Pinch Points
- Elena experiences same phenomena Archer described (lights, phantom ships)
- Is it real or isolation-induced delusion?
- Discovery: Several lighthouse keepers across generations vanished
- All investigated "The Perseverance"

#### Crisis/All Is Lost (End of Act 2)
- Elena's own grip on reality questioned
- Evidence she's been sleepwalking, like Archer described in journal
- She finds her own handwriting in places she doesn't remember
- Must choose: abandon investigation for her sanity, or continue

### Act 3 - Resolution (25% / ~21,000 words)
**Goal**: Climax, resolution, denouement

#### Climax
- Elena goes to the coordinates during a fog (like Dylan did)
- Discovers the truth about "The Perseverance"
- Confronts what happened to Dylan
- The journal's final pages reveal Archer's fate

#### Falling Action
- Elena returns to lighthouse
- Makes decision about her future
- Resolves relationship with her past

#### Resolution
- New lighthouse keeper arrives OR Elena embraces her role differently
- The mystery is solved but some questions remain
- Elena finds peace with Dylan's fate
- Closes arc about isolation vs. connection

## Major Plot Threads

### Thread 1: The Journal Mystery
- Setup: Journal discovered, ship doesn't exist in records
- Development: More entries reveal impossible events
- Resolution: Truth about "The Perseverance" revealed

### Thread 2: Dylan's Disappearance
- Setup: Elena's brother vanished three years ago
- Development: He investigated same mystery
- Resolution: Elena learns what happened to him

### Thread 3: Elena's Isolation
- Setup: Self-imposed exile at lighthouse
- Development: Investigation forces her to reconnect
- Resolution: Decision about her future

### Thread 4: Reality vs. Delusion
- Setup: Isolated lighthouse keeper reading old journal
- Development: Elena experiences same phenomena as Archer
- Resolution: Determination of what's real

## Open Questions
- Is "The Perseverance" real or allegorical?
- What exactly happened to Captain Archer?
- Did Dylan die or disappear by choice?
- Will Elena remain at the lighthouse?
- Are the supernatural elements real or psychological?
